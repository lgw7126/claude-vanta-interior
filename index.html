How We Work

A rigorous process refined over eight years. Clear milestones, zero guesswork.

Three distinct design directions, each with mood boards, material palettes, and spatial layouts.

We believe the best interiors are never finished — they evolve.

Founded in 2017 in New York, VANTA was built on a conviction that interior design should begin with the person, not the trend. Our team of eight senior designers has worked on residences, hotels, and private clubs across four continents.

We don't take on more than four projects at a time. That's not a constraint — it's our quality guarantee.
